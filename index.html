Comprehensive Services Guide

Detailed information on every treatment we offer to restore and maintain your oral health.

Preventive & Restorative

Professional teeth cleaning removes stubborn plaque and tartar that brushing misses, preventing gum disease. For decay, our dental fillings use tooth-colored composite resins that blend seamlessly with your enamel, restoring function with minimally invasive techniques.

Dentures & Extractions

Custom dentures provide a natural-looking solution for missing multiple teeth, restoring chewing ability. Professional tooth extractions are necessary for impacted wisdom teeth, utilizing advanced sedation for a painless procedure.

Orthodontics & Implants

Clear aligners offer a discreet way to straighten teeth with custom, removable trays. For severe misalignments, traditional dental braces use high-quality brackets to apply precise pressure. Dental implants are the gold standard for permanent replacement, surgically placing titanium posts to prevent bone loss.

Endodontics

If a tooth is infected, root canal therapy carefully removes the diseased pulp and seals the tooth, saving it from extraction and preserving your natural smile.
